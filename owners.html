Leadership & Team

Capt. Cody Betzer

President, Managing Partner

Cody, born in Tucson, AZ, is a seasoned professional with a diverse background in private security, intelligence, cyber security, software development, and robotics. With over 15 years of experience in private security and intelligence, and over 20 years in cyber security, software development, and robotics, Cody brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to his roles.

Early Life and Entrepreneurship

Cody was born into a law enforcement family and developed a passion for protecting others from a young age. While his initial inclination was towards software development, Cody's desire for action led him to enroll in executive protection and pistol classes. This experience ignited his interest in security and personal protection.

After running a successful technology company, Revolusys, which catered to the oil and gas industry, Cody decided to pursue his passion for security full-time. He served as the head of security and personal protection for a major hip hop artist, traveling across the country and gaining firsthand experience in VIP protection.

Entrepreneurship and Game Development

Following the closure of Revolusys, Cody took a hiatus and eventually founded Desert Rain Interactive Studios. The studio became a 3rd party game developer for Bohemia Interactive Studios, working on the popular title "Arma 3." Cody's unique combination of passions led him to create "The PMC Simulator," an add-on that provided players with a private security company management experience within the game. The add-on gained popularity internationally and remains highly regarded.

Return to Security and Training

Feeling a deep calling to return to protecting others, Cody pursued formal training in anti-terrorism and became certified as an anti-terrorism officer. He then transitioned to working in intelligence gathering and participated in operations focused on combating narcotics and human trafficking along the U.S.-Mexico border. Cody's expertise in computer technology, electronics, and physics enhanced his effectiveness in these operations.

In 2020, Cody decided to share his skills and experiences by becoming an instructor. He became certified as an NRA instructor for pistol, rifle, shotgun, and personal protection, as well as a DPS unarmed and armed guard instructor. He opened Attanaya Warrior Institute, a state-of-the-art firearms simulation training facility, offering immersive and realistic training experiences.

Privateer Security Forces

In late 2020, Cody merged his passion for training with his security expertise and founded Privateer Security Forces. Serving as the owner and managing partner, Cody leads the company, offering comprehensive security services. He also serves as the owner and commander of Privateer Academy, leveraging his extensive knowledge to train the next generation of protectors.

Lt. Cdr. Kenneth Koepke

Director of Training and Integration

Early Life and Military Service

Koepke embarked on his journey by graduating from high school in Tucson, where he laid the foundation for his future endeavors. In 1995, he enlisted in the United States Marine Corps and served as an infantryman with the esteemed 3rd Battalion, 4th Marine Division until 2000. During his time in the Marines, Koepke undertook rigorous training and earned multiple certifications, including the Mcmwtc certification, Mcmwtc Survival certification, M.O.U.T (Military Operations in Urban Terrain) Operation Instructions certification, Deep Water Survival certification, Hilo Operations survival certification, JWTC (Jungle Warfare Training Center) certification, and many more.

Law Enforcement Career

After completing his service in the Marine Corps, Koepke transitioned to a new chapter in his life, joining the Department of Corrections in 2001. With an unwavering dedication to maintaining public safety, he spent over two decades in law enforcement, honing his expertise in various areas. As a firearms instructor, Koepke ensured that his fellow officers received the training needed to effectively and safely handle firearms. His knowledge and skill extended to tactical support, where he qualified as a member of the Tactical Support Unit. Furthermore, his precision and sharpshooting ability led him to achieve the distinction of becoming a qualified sniper.

Koepke's proficiency extended beyond firearms as he also became certified in Taser instruction and munitions, attending the Glock Armorer School, Remington Armorer School, and Pepper Ball Armorer School to enhance his understanding of weapon maintenance and functionality. Additionally, he completed training in fugitive apprehension, CTS (Combined Tactical Systems), and became a Complex Armorer proficient in maintaining a range of equipment. Koepke's dedication to law enforcement and his continuous pursuit of knowledge led him to achieve a PR24 (Side Handle Baton) instructor certification.

Martial Arts Expertise

Throughout his career, Koepke's passion for martial arts remained a constant. In 2007, he became a certified instructor in Krav Maga through the USKMA (United States Krav Maga Association), reaching the rank of 2nd Dan. This accomplishment reflects his dedication to mastering this practical and effective self-defense system. Moreover, Koepke's commitment to martial arts continued with his involvement in Alpha Krav Maga, where he achieved the rank of 3rd Dan.

Conclusion

Kenneth Koepke's life has been marked by his unwavering commitment to service and expertise. From his years in the Marine Corps to his extensive career in law enforcement and his proficiency in martial arts, he exemplifies discipline, dedication, and a desire to make a difference. Through his continuous pursuit of knowledge and honing of skills, Koepke has left an indelible mark on those he has trained and served alongside, embodying the spirit of a true leader and lifelong learner.

Our Team

The heart and soul of PSF

Privateers are a rare and unique breed. We are small, we are few, but we are insanely mighty. PSF employs a diverse team of professionals, hailing from all walks of life. We are an equal opportunity employer, and since we train in house, from the ground up, we have access to a wide pool of potential candidates whose only initial requirement to apply is courage, grit, and determination to be a part of something bigger than themselves. We are a youthful team, strong and able. Each and every person who calls themselves a "Privateer" has earned the title through intensive training and desire to succeed - to be the best. That's not to say that none of our team come from military and law enforcement backgrounds, as most do! But for every expert who has 15 years of combat experience, we have a student learning what they know, and applying it to our operations here.
