Missing something or someone?

Privateer Security Forces BOLO services enlist our patrolling privateers to help search for your missing persons or property. Missing a bike, car or other property? Send us all the information related to the property, and we will join the effort to locate your missing goods. Should we locate your property, we will stay with the property to the best of our lawful ability, gather all available information, and notify law enforcement and the owner. If it is safe and lawful for us to assume control of the property, we will do so. We offer missing property BOLO services at a small base fee + bounty rate.

Missing persons are another service we offer, and due to the nature of the service, we offer missing persons BOLOs free of charge. Simply share all available information with us, and we will join the search for your missing person to the best of our lawful ability. We do not detain missing persons! If we are able to locate your missing person, we may attempt to make contact with that person if it's safe to do so. If the person does not agree to accompany us, we are required to inform law enforcement and the client of the encounter, but are unable to detain missing persons unlawfully.
