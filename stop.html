STOP Team

Privateer Security Forces is proud to announce the formation of our Special Tactics and Operations (STOP) team. STOP is a highly trained and specialized team of security professionals that are trained to handle the most difficult and dangerous security situations. STOP is comprised of former military and law enforcement personnel that have extensive experience in high risk security operations. STOP is available to handle any security situation that may arise. STOP is available for both short and long term contracts.

Operations

The STOP team executes precision operations in challenging urban environments, specializing in the following areas:

  • Urban Reconnaissance and Reclamation: Expertise in the strategic entry and clearing of abandoned structures, ensuring secure and safe reclamation of these areas.
  • Private Property Defense: Specialized in the removal of unauthorized occupants, including squatters, from private properties, restoring rightful control to the owners.
  • Homeless Encampment Displacement: Efficiently disbanding and clearing homeless camps situated on private lands, executed with strategic planning and adherence to legal protocols.
  • Narcotic Stronghold Neutralization: Identifying and dismantling drug dens on private properties, utilizing intelligence-driven tactics to target these hotbeds of illegal activity.
  • Checkpoint Control: Establishing vehicle checkpoints on private properties, employing a dual approach of voluntary search compliance or access denial to ensure security and control.
  • Covert Surveillance Operations: Conducting discreet surveillance on private properties to monitor and report drug-related activities, leveraging advanced techniques for intelligence gathering.
  • Dominance in High-Threat Zones: Employing a strategic show of overwhelming lawful force in areas plagued by drug-related and other forms of violence, targeting and neutralizing the most notorious drug dens and problematic apartment complexes.

Our STOP team is adept in these critical operations, ensuring our clients' properties are safeguarded and restored to safety and order with military precision and effectiveness.
