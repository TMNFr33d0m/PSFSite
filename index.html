Privateer Security Forces offers traditional and specialty physical security solutions that include the latest technology and personnel with training that exceeds the state minimums by orders of magnitude. Our academy alumni earn the prestigious title of "PRIVATEER" and take pride in their status as community leaders.

PSF prides itself on being well educated, well equipped, and highly organized. We are the only security company in Arizona with an active intelligence division! We utilize cutting edge technology to our maximum benefit, and our efficiency and internal capabilities benefit as a result. Utilizing drone technology, we can provide our Privateers with high tech surveillance solutions that allow them to perform their security functions. Utilizing radio over mobile technology, our organization enjoys company-wide communications, blue force tracking, group messaging and more! As a direct result, our clients enjoy better security services from better informed Privateers, and a scalable security force that can amass and disperse as needed to maintain peace and order.

Privateer diversifies it's operations by engaging in the business of developing quality security operations software that our clients and Privateers can count on. Our flagship software product, "Full Sail", is a powerful security operations suite that provides security professionals with important tools and other data required to safely handle situations, limit liability and assist in building strong legal cases that accomplish real results for our clients.
