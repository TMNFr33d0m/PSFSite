Posted Security Services:

Posted security services are the traditional mode of security most people know about. A Privateer is contracted to be on premises at your location for a set shift, on a set schedule. Most of the time, you will have the same Privateer on your premises, and he or she may perform special duties that add to the productivity of your business or streamline the processes at your facility, including searches, locks / unlocks, access control, surveillance monitoring, peace keeping operations, safety operations, and more. Your Privateer will have access to our data systems for time keeping, reporting and activity tracking purposes, and you will be able to access this information via the client portal.

PSF offers two modes of posted privateers:

Uniformed Privateer
  • Powerful authoritative presence to deter disorder
  • Equipped with radio communication device, creating a professional audible deterrent and allowing the Privateer to call for assistance and scale the security force as needed in response to a threat
  • May openly wear body armor and carry defensive devices
  • May function as customer service based on uniformed status
  • May deploy automated technologies to assist in premises surveillance
  • May use a vehicle, computer, and other visible
Plain Clothes Privateer
  • Covert presence to interdict disorder
  • Equipped with covert radio communication device, allowing the Privateer to call for assistance and scale the security force as needed in response to a threat
  • Conceals all body armor and defensive devices
  • Maintains a highly covert presence to avoid detection and identification as a security presence
