Leading the way to a safer community!

Security Forces is committed to our community, and in the course of our commitment to our community, we recognize certain functions are excpected of community leaders in uniform, whether they are sworn law enforcement officers or security professionals entrusted by the community to provide protection to private entities.

To this end, we at Privateer Security Forces pledge to provide the folllowing services to our community FREE OF CHARGE and at our own expense:

  • Road Emergency Response: In the event we are the first on the scene to a car accident, we will use our state of the art vehicles and lighting systems to secure the crash scene and prevent additional collisions. We will then provide 'good samartian' medical care to any injured on the scene with our on board medical kits and standard first aid certification. Finally, we will utilize on board equipment to direct and maintain the safe flow of traffic until emergency responders are able to arrive and take control of the scene. We will then provide all pertinent information to authorities and remain on scene only if requested to do so by law enforcement or emergency personnell.
  • Natural Disaster Response: In the event of a natural disaster that effects the public en masse (flooding, for example), PSF personnel will suspend company operations to provide community support wherever they are. This includes directing traffic, leading or participating in rescue efforts, providing medical care, humanitarian aid and support to emergency services.
  • Violent Incident Intervention: In the event we are the first on scene to an act of violence, including physical assault, active shooters, rioting, looting and other activities that directly and actively put others in imminent danger of serious physical injury or death, we will stop any threats and restore peace and order to the community.

We certify that we do not require any payment for these services, nor do we require any special authority to perform these functions as trained leaders in our community. These are not exclusively law enforcement functions, and are duties that should be taken upon by capable individuals in times of crisis. Therefore, we will perform these duties as our commitment to our community safety. That is our guarantee.
