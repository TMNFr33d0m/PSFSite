Employee Selection Process

Application Submission

Interested candidates must submit their applications through the designated channels provided by the company.
To apply, visit our online application portal.

Program Schedule

The Elite Military-like Selection Process will be conducted multiple times a year, as determined by the company's staffing requirements and business needs.

Physical Fitness Assessment

Candidates will undergo a rigorous physical fitness assessment to evaluate their strength, endurance, and agility. The assessment includes a series of fitness tests and obstacle courses designed to challenge candidates' physical capabilities.

Mental and Cognitive Assessments

Candidates will participate in various mental and cognitive assessments to evaluate their critical thinking skills, problem-solving abilities, and decision-making under pressure.

Leadership and Teamwork Exercises

Candidates will engage in leadership and teamwork exercises to assess their ability to work collaboratively and lead effectively in challenging situations. These exercises may include simulated missions or scenarios that require candidates to communicate, strategize, and make collective decisions.

Feedback and Debriefing

Privateer Security Forces values transparency and fairness. Candidates who participated in the selection process but were not selected will be offered constructive feedback and debriefing sessions to help them understand areas for improvement.
